Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pi is the brains of Tingbot. You might already have one in a drawer somewhere! We support Raspberry Pi B+, 2 and 3.

Tingbot OS

Tingbot runs our own operating system so everything is ready to go right out the box.

Screen module

Our custom touch screen attaches to the Pi through the GPIO pins.

Button board

Our button board provides four old-school clicky buttons that you can use in your apps.

Case

The caps hold the Raspberry Pi and screen together and the wrap packages it up neatly - you can even make your own!
